# RetailPluse-Ecommerce-Analytics.
An end-to-end data analytics project analyzing an e-commerce dataset to uncover insights about revenue trends, shipping delays, product performance, and customer lifetime value (CLV). The project uses Python, SQL, and exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify operational bottlenecks and business growth opportunities.

📌 Business Problem

RetailPulse has experienced rapid growth in customer orders, but management is facing several operational and strategic challenges:

Some products sell extremely well while others receive almost no orders.

Shipping delays are increasing.

Discounts are applied inconsistently.

Revenue growth is slowing despite increasing order volume.

This project aims to analyze sales, shipping performance, and customer behavior to provide actionable insights.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze revenue performance and sales trends

Identify shipping delays and operational bottlenecks

Discover top-performing and non-performing products

Measure Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Provide data-driven business recommendations

💡 Business Recommendations
